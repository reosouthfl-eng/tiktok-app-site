Privacy Policy

Last updated: September 1, 2025

This policy describes how we handle information for our TikTok integration tools (“Service”).

What we collect

  • TikTok account data provided by TikTok during OAuth (e.g., open_id).
  • OAuth tokens (access and refresh tokens) to act on your behalf after you grant permission.
  • Operational data needed to upload or manage your videos (file size, captions you provide, etc.).
  • Basic diagnostics (server logs and error logs) to troubleshoot issues.

How we use data

  • Authenticate you with TikTok and maintain your login session.
  • Upload drafts or post videos to your TikTok account when you request it.
  • Check post status and show results back to you.

Data sharing

We do not sell your data. We share data only with service providers (e.g., TikTok APIs) as required to perform the Service, or when required by law.

Data retention

OAuth tokens are stored securely and retained until you revoke access, your tokens expire, or you request deletion. Operational data is kept only as long as needed for the Service.

Your choices

  • You may revoke the app’s access in your TikTok account settings.
  • To request access or deletion, contact us at the email below.

Security

We use reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your data. No system is 100% secure.
